Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

The short version: browsing and downloading need no account and set no tracking cookies. We run no ads, use no third-party trackers, and never sell data. The only personal data we hold is the profile of contributors who choose to sign in.

1. Visitors (no account)

  • No account needed to browse, search, or download anything.
  • Analytics: we use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is cookieless and collects no personal information — only aggregate page counts and performance metrics.
  • Server logs: our host (Cloudflare) processes IP addresses transiently to serve requests and prevent abuse, as any web host does. We do not build profiles from them.
  • All browser tools on this site (color pickers, generators, editors) run entirely in your browser; what you create in them never leaves your device unless you explicitly upload it.

2. Contributors (signed in)

To upload assets you sign in with GitHub or Google (OAuth). We then store:

  • your public profile from the provider: name, username, avatar, and email address;
  • a session cookie (__Host-fds_mcp_session) — strictly necessary for keeping you signed in, HTTPS-only, not used for tracking;
  • your creator profile (handle, bio, links you choose to add) and the assets you upload, both of which are public by design;
  • upload timestamps and counts, used only for rate limiting and abuse prevention.

We never see or store your GitHub/Google password, and we do not post or act on your behalf.

3. What we don't do

  • No advertising, no ad networks, no third-party trackers or pixels.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing personal data with anyone.
  • No marketing emails — we don't email you at all unless you contact us first.

4. Where data lives

Everything runs on Cloudflare (Pages, Workers, R2 storage, KV). Data may be processed in any Cloudflare data center worldwide. Note that uploaded assets are dedicated to the public domain (see Terms) — once published and copied by others, the asset itself cannot be recalled, though we can always remove it from our catalog.

5. Your rights

You can view and edit your creator profile in the Console, and delete your own assets at any time. To delete your account and profile data entirely, or to ask what we hold about you, open an issue on GitHub or email us, and we'll action it promptly. EU/UK visitors: the legal basis for contributor data is contract (providing the service you signed up for); for transient logs, legitimate interest (security).

6. Children

The service is not directed at children under 13, and contributor accounts require a GitHub or Google account, which have their own age requirements.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date changes when we do. Material changes will be noted on this page.

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub. See also our Terms & License.
